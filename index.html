OUR MISSION

The Metal Hall of Fame is a non-profit organization dedicated to enshrining forever, those Iconic musicians and music industry executives who are responsible for making Rock and Metal music what it is today. Their contribution to the genre is invaluable, and they keep inspiring fans throughout the world, from generation to generation.

The Metal Hall of Fame does The Hall of Heavy Metal History Gala on annual basis in Anaheim California. In addition, The Metal Hall of Fame is part of the D.A.D. Program (Drums and Disabilities) to help children fight disabilities on Globally, and joins with the Ronnie James Dio Stand Up And Shout Cancer Fund.
